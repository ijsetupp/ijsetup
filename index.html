Canon IJ printer Guide for Driver Installation

Follow the below guide and set up your Cannon printer

  1. Download Drivers – Visitij.start.canon and download the Cannon driver.
  2. Install and Select connection type – Double-tap on file and select wireless or USB connection.
  3. Turn ON the Cannon printer – Press the button to turn on the printer.
  4. Connect WiFi – On a wireless connection, press and hold the WiFI alarm to connect.
  5. Flashing blue and green lamps – If you find this situation, it means the printer is connected to WiFi.
  6. Agree on the terms to install – Read and agree to the license terms of Cannon set up in order to install.
  7. Finish the Cannon Printer Setup – Tap on Complete to finish the final step.
